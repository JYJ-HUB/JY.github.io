3D printing method of mannequin using human body features and bounding box

/ KR1020210000000A /

Summary
The point of this invention is to automatically explore the human feature points of the mannequin considering the size of the 3D printer output, and to split and 3D print the body of a large human-sized mannequin through a bounding box.
